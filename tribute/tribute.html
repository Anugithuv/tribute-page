Here is a time line of KOBE BRYANT life

  • 1978 - Kobe Bean Bryant born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1985 - Growing up in Italy alongside two athletic older sisters, Shaya and Sharia, Bryant was an avid player of both basketball and soccer.
  • 1991 - When the family returned to Philadelphia, Bryant joined the Lower Merion High School basketball team, leading it to the state championships four years in a row.
  • 1992 - With an eye on the NBA, he also started working out with the 76ers. Bryant decided to go straight to the NBA from high school.
  • 1996 - He was selected by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th overall pick of the NBA draft and was subsequently traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1996-19997 - As a rookie, Bryant mostly came off the bench behind guards Eddie Jones and Nick Van Exel.At the time, he was the second-youngest player ever to play in an NBA game (18 years, 72 days) and also became the youngest NBA starter (18 years, 158 days).Initially, Bryant played limited minutes, but as the season continued, he began to see some more playing time.
  • 1997 - During the All-Star weekend, Bryant participated in the Rookie Challenge and won the Slam Dunk Contest, becoming the youngest dunk champion at the age of 18.
  • 1999 - Bryant's fortunes improved when Phil Jackson took over as coach of the Lakers in 1999.After years of steady improvement, Bryant became one of the premier shooting guards in the league, earning appearances in the league's All-NBA,All-Star, and All-Defensive teams.
  • 2000-2002 - kobe won three championships consecutively.
  • 2001 - kobe married Vanessa Laine at St. Edward the Confessor Catholic Church in Dana Point, California.
